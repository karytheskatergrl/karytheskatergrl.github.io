Geosphere - boardgame project

This was my graduation project for University. It was the most complex project at that time. From sketching boardgame ideas to creating the logo, overall graphics, the box, boardgame rules. This is  a tiny amount of the whole process I went through. Enjoy!

More about this project

3D visual representation of how the game works. The pieces are made out of a thin layer of metal. The sphere has magnets all around so that the pieces will stick to the board. Players take turns on placing pieces on the board, trying to get the highest amount of points out of each move.

Made a couple of silicone negatives of the 3D printed piece to pour the others. The poured pieces are epoxy. These is a 2mm middle triangle area left to get filled with magnets. A small empty area is left on the sides of the magnet so you can easily lift the pieces of the board.
